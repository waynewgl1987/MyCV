# 📖 Dual N Back Training — Introduction

## What is Dual N-Back?

Dual N-Back is a cognitive training task recognized as one of the most effective working memory training methods in cognitive science. It has been validated by hundreds of peer-reviewed scientific papers showing significant positive effects on fluid intelligence and working memory.

## How to Play?

Each round, you see a highlighted square on a 3×3 grid (position) and hear a spoken letter (sound). You need to remember these stimuli and determine whether the current stimulus matches the one N rounds back:

- **Position matches** → Press "Position Match"
- **Sound matches** → Press "Sound Match"
- **Neither matches** → Do nothing, wait for the next round

## Passing Criteria

- Accuracy ≥ 90% → Full score
- Accuracy ≥ 80% → Half score
- Accuracy < 80% → Keep practicing

Accuracy = (Correct Hits + Correct Rejections) ÷ Total Judgments × 100%

## Benefits

- Improves working memory capacity
- Enhances fluid intelligence
- Positively impacts cognitive development in children
- Helps slow cognitive decline in older adults
- Improves focus and attention control
- Better handling of complex tasks in daily life

## Scientific Basis

In 2008, Susanne Jaeggi et al. published a landmark study in the *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS)* demonstrating that Dual N-Back training can significantly improve fluid intelligence. Subsequent research has confirmed these findings, especially for cognitive development in children and adolescents. Results can be seen with just 15–20 minutes of daily training for 20 consecutive days.

> 💡 Recommended: Train for 15 minutes daily, stick with it for 20 days!
